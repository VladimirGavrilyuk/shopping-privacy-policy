Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 14, 2024

Summary: Shopping app does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information. All your data stays on your device.

1. Information Collection and Use

Shopping app is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal information from our users.

2. Data Storage

All data created within the app is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage mechanisms:

  • Shopping lists and items
  • User preferences (theme, language)
  • App settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

3. Permissions

The app does not require any special Android permissions beyond basic app functionality. The app does NOT request access to:

  • Internet connection
  • Location services
  • Camera or microphone
  • Contacts or phone data
  • External storage (beyond app-specific storage)

4. Third-Party Services

Shopping app does NOT use any third-party services, analytics, advertising networks, or tracking tools. The app operates completely offline.

5. Advertising

This app is completely ad-free. We do not display any advertisements or use any advertising networks.

6. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any information, our app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

7. Data Security

Your data is stored securely on your device using Android's standard security mechanisms. Since data never leaves your device, it is protected by your device's security features (lock screen, encryption, etc.).

8. Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear list" function within the app
  • Uninstalling the app from your device
  • Clearing app data through Android Settings → Apps → Shopping → Storage → Clear Data

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Since the app does not collect data, changes will typically be minor clarifications.

10. Open Source

Shopping app respects user privacy and transparency. The app's code follows best practices for privacy and security.
