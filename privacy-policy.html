Privacy Policy

Shopping App

Last Updated: January 2025

Important: This app collects device identifiers to prevent fraud and manage device limits. By using this app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Shopping ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Device Identifiers

We collect the following device-related information:

  • Android ID (Device ID): A unique identifier tied to your device hardware
  • Device Name: The manufacturer and model of your device (e.g., "Samsung Galaxy S23")
  • Device Model: Technical model information
  • Last Active Timestamp: When you last used the app

2.2 Authentication Data

If you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Firebase UID: Your unique Firebase authentication identifier
  • Email Address: Your Google account email (for signed-in users only)
  • Display Name: Your Google account name

2.3 Shopping List Data

We store your shopping lists and items:

  • List names and creation dates
  • Shopping items, quantities, and categories
  • Completion status of items

2.4 App Settings

  • Language preference
  • Theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Sound settings

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Fraud Prevention & Device Management

We use Device ID (Android ID) to:

  • Prevent fraud and abuse: Block malicious devices and prevent spam
  • Enforce device limits: Anonymous users are limited to 1 device; Google users can use up to 3 devices
  • Manage active sessions: Limit concurrent sessions to prevent account sharing abuse
  • Detect suspicious behavior: Identify unusual patterns of usage

3.2 Data Synchronization

For Google Sign-In users, we use Firebase UID to:

  • Sync shopping lists across your authorized devices
  • Maintain data consistency when you use multiple devices
  • Provide seamless access to your data

3.3 App Functionality

  • Store and display your shopping lists
  • Apply your preferred settings (language, theme, sounds)
  • Enable offline functionality with local storage

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: By using the app, you consent to data collection as described
  • Legitimate Interests: Fraud prevention and service security
  • Contractual Necessity: To provide the service you requested

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where We Store Your Data

  • Local Device: Shopping lists are stored locally in SQLite database
  • Firebase Firestore: Device information and synced data (encrypted in transit)
  • Firebase Authentication: User authentication tokens and profile data

5.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmitted to Firebase is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Firebase Security Rules restrict access to authorized users only
  • Device IDs are hashed and stored securely
  • We do not store credit card or payment information

5.3 Data Retention

  • Anonymous users: Device data retained until you uninstall the app or request deletion
  • Google Sign-In users: Data retained while you have an active account
  • Blocked devices: Block status retained indefinitely for fraud prevention

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

6.1 We Do NOT Share Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

6.2 Service Providers

We use the following trusted service providers:

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation.

  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format

9.2 Appeal Process
